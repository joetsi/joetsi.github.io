Accommodation

Unfortunately there is limited accommodation within Vicq sur Breuilh itself, so we would recommend staying in one of the following three nearby villages for the night of the wedding (Pierre-Buffière, Magnac-Bourg or Saint-Germain les Belles). Check out the links below for suggestions of where to stay (all sites in English):

Pierre-Buffière (accessible by train)

Millevaches National Park (Walking, camping and huge lake (Vassivière) for water sports)

musee-adriendubouche

1 - 3 hours’ drive

  • Auvergne Volcanoes National Park (Impressive scenery, good for hiking)
  • Bordeaux (Nice architecture, great wine…)
  • Gascon Landes National Park (Beautiful Atlantic beaches, surfing, home of Europe’s largest sand dune!)
  • Toulouse (Occupying a special place in our hearts… pretty and unusual (for France) brick architecture, lots of great cafes/bars…)
  • Loire-valley castles (Pretend to be a King or Queen for the day)

Further afield

  • Paris (You’ve heard it all before)
  • Montpellier (Pretty city on Mediterranean coast)
  • Lyon (France’s third biggest city, gateway to the Alps)
  • Cevennes National Park (Rugged scenery and dramatic gorges, great for hiking and canoeing)
